Land of Zykola

The book is a fantasy novel by the Egyptian writer Amr Abdel Hamid. It tells the story of Khaled, a young man from a village called Al-Bahoufrik, who finds himself in a strange land called Zikula, where people trade with units of intelligence instead of money. In Zikula, the poorest (or the least intelligent) person is sacrificed every year in a ritual called the Zikula Day, where they have to play a game of shooting at a small model of themselves. Khaled tries to find a way out of Zikula and discovers that his father and brother had visited this land before him and left behind a mysterious book that contains clues about a secret passage. He also meets Asil, a doctor who helps him in his quest and falls in love with him. The book is full of adventure, suspense and mystery.
